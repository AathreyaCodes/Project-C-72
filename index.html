Our Campus

School Auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school Auditorium is a huge room perfect for our shining star students to perform a wide variety of their talents, all honed through experience. From Poem recitals, to dance numbers, to debates, our Auditorium provides the perfect stage to let the world witness the talent that's hidden within you.



School Library

Was built in 1980

The school Library provides our young students opportunity to learn and grow through a wide range of books on various subjects. It offers a quiet learning space for the students to develop a new skill, hone an existing skill, learn a new branch of science, ace their tests or just escape into the fictional world of books.



School Church

Was established in 1972

The school church is a place for the students of our school to express their devotion to Jesus and practice their choirs, etc.
